# Top 10 PowerShell Fails

![Top 10 PowerShell Fails/ Hania Bielawska](Fail.png)

You can find "slides"/ demos in separate [GitHub repo](https://github.com/bielawb/powershell-fail/tree/master).

## Abstract

As a father, I know very well: just because you love something doesn’t mean you should ignore its flaws. In this session, I’m going to share my top PowerShell “beefs”, both historical and current. I’m going to touch on concepts that looked good on paper but either never reached their full potential or got butchered to the point where nobody wants to touch them anymore. Feel free to bring your own grieves!
