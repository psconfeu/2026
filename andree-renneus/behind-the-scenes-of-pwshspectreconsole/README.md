# Behind the Scenes of PwshSpectreConsole

## Abstract

PwshSpectreConsole brings rich, interactive output to PowerShell, but what makes it work?
In this session, I’ll walk through some of the internals and share the challenges of building advanced rendering in PowerShell.
I’ll highlight a few design patterns and clever solutions that make the module possible and keep it reliable.
By the end, you’ll have a better understanding of how the module works and ideas you can use in your own projects.
